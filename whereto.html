What's in store for 2019? There's Another Big Trip Planned!

This time we will be staying in Northern Europe. We start in Bruges, in Belgium and head south on a two thousand five hundred mile round trip through Belgium, Luxombourg, Germany, Switzerland and down to Italy, where we will spend some time in Florence, and looping back up through Eastern France to Dunkirk and back to the UK. We will spend time in the Alpes, the Ardeche, both Belgium and France sides, visit Geneva and the Italian coastline. Five weeks in Europe... What else should we do?
